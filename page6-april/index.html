The Month of Confession...

April 7th. That’s when I finally said what I had been holding in for a long time. I spoke honestly, without expectations — just truth. She didn’t feel the same. It wasn’t cruel. It wasn’t loud. But it was enough to change something inside me. I stayed quiet after that. Not because I was angry — but because I was learning how to carry disappointment without turning it into bitterness.
